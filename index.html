大连梭鱼湾足球场

Posted on 2023-07-31

Membrane structure丨Dalian Barracuda Bay football field

The project covers a total area of about 26.5 hectares, with a total construction area of about 136,000 square meters, positioned as a 63,000-seat special football field, and is the only seaside football stadium in China surrounded by the sea on three sides. Dalian’s first professional football stadium can further improve the taste of the city and serve more fans to become a new landmark in Dalian.

The design of the project is inspired by “waves and conch”, and the design concept is “colorful waves”. The façade of the building is set in the color of “Ocean Blue”, the ceiling is white “Blue Ocean White Clouds” and the finish symbolizes the form of sparkling water, integrating Dalian’s marine culture and football spirit. At night, the whole stadium is illuminated and romantic. The lighting system creates layers through intricate and regular arrangements, highlighting romantic seaside tones. It presents a beautiful visual feast for the people of the city.

The roof of the project adopts PTFE membrane of about 40,000 square meters, forming a wave shape. The façade is composed of ETFE cable membrane structure and surrounding cable membrane floodlighting system. Among them, the façade adopts ETFE membrane of nearly 40,000 square meters, including 9 kinds of blue membrane and 7823 diaphragms, which is currently the largest membrane structure coverage area of professional football stadiums in the world, and the project with the largest single-layer ETFE membrane structure.

The overall grandstand seats of the stadium are made of six colors, gray and blue, gradually fading from the low area to the high area, and the use of curves and gradient techniques reflects the tumbling pattern of the waves, showing the active and lively atmosphere of the stadium and the distinctive characteristics of the ocean city.

Dalian Barracuda Bay Football Stadium project covers a total area of about 26.5 hectares, with a total construction area of about 136,000 square meters, positioned as a 63,000-seat special football field, is the only seaside football stadium surrounded by the sea on three sides in China, and the first professional football stadium in Dalian.

China Construction Electronics undertakes the construction of 25 subsystems such as the digital platform and intelligent operation center of the venue, adopts mature comprehensive solutions for smart venues, takes intelligence, visualization, and building operation and maintenance management as the core, and realizes refined management of the venue network and data through the Internet of Things, big data, cloud, AI and other technologies, benchmarks against international competition standards, and effectively improves the efficiency of hosting the competition.

Based on the intelligent scenario and intelligent planning of the professional football stadium in Barracuda Bay, China State Construction Electronics adopts the idea of “layered decoupling” to build the overall architecture of the intelligent stadium in Barracuda Bay, including intelligent facility layer, intelligent platform, intelligent application and other levels, integrating the application systems in different levels of the football stadium, breaking the information “island”, and realizing the integration of various business functions, data sharing and the overall management of various system operation processe
