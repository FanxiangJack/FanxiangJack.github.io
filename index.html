Dalian Earthquake

Posted on 2023-08-29

At more than 6 o ‘clock in the evening on August 23, my grandmother called me and said that Dalian earthquake! I couldn’t help but worry about my grandparents’ safety. My grandmother knew I liked geography and asked me two questions: Why did the earthquake happen? Dalian is near the sea. Will the earthquake cause tsunami?

I hurried to find out about it on the Internet. A 4.6-magnitude earthquake hit Plandian district (39.42 degrees North latitude, 122.20 degrees east longitude) at a depth of 8 kilometers on Aug 23 at 18:19 PM in Dalian, Liaoning province. 23 at 18:40, Pulandian district aftershock, magnitude 2.8, focal depth of 10 kilometers, epicenter at 39.42 degrees north latitude, 122.18 degrees east longitude.

Why did this earthquake happen? I consulted the relevant data and learned that the occurrence of this earthquake may be based on the following reasons: This earthquake may be a tectonic earthquake, or associated with the crustal movement of the fault zone. As far as the epicenter is concerned, the area where the earthquake occurred is probably located on the Jinzhou fault zone in the northern section of the Tancheng-Lu fault zone (a main fault zone in a series of NE-trending giant fault systems on the East Asian continent). However, the specific cause of the earthquake remains to be explained by the authority of the relevant departments.

Dalian is near the sea. Will the earthquake cause tsunami? Experts believe that the tsunami originated from a strong undersea earthquake, and the formation of the ocean plate to the subduction of the land plate, the formation of subduction tectonic zone, may cause tsunami. It is commonly understood that the earthquake that causes a tsunami occurs in the sea, and the epicenter will not form a tsunami on land.

After looking up the information, I called my grandmother and answered her two questions. I told her not to be afraid, that according to the official analysis of the seismic monitoring data, the likelihood of a destructive earthquake in the near future is unlikely.

Dalian Jinshitan Geological Museum Tour

Posted on 2023-08-29

On July 24, 2023, I visited Dalian Jinshitan National Geological Museum. It is located at the entrance of the beautiful Jinshitan core scenic spot, with eight exhibition halls, which help me understand the geological knowledge of the earth’s evolution and development process.

In the exhibition hall, I gained a lot. The most interested is the Earth Room. I first followed the “axis of time” to travel the process of biological evolution and understand the laws of biological evolution. Next, I learned about “Earth memory.” The earth is changing all the time under the surface of unchanging. The rocks beneath our feet, like cells, record the life of the earth, containing precious traces of geological evolution. The one that impressed me the most was minerals. The crust is divided into continental crust and oceanic crust. The continental crust is composed of sedimentary layers, granite layers and basalt layers. Minerals are the basic building blocks of rocks and ores. The formation of minerals cannot be separated from endogenesis, exogenesis and metamorphism. The mineral world is colorful, with red, yellow, green, purple, orange, white and so on. Different minerals have different colors, and even the same mineral has different colors. Why do minerals have different colors? The main reason is the selective absorption of visible light by the object. The main factors that determine the color of minerals are the chemical composition, structural structure, and the degree of oxidation. Finally, I looked at a few other showrooms.

After the visit, I developed a keen interest in minerals. There are countless mysteries hidden in the world, I want to learn more knowledge, uncover their mystery, and contribute to society! Embark on a journey of discovery! More gas!

Dalian Barracuda Bay Football Stadium

Posted on 2023-08-29

“China’s first professional football stadium facing the sea on three sides”, “Dalian New Landmark Building” With these labels, Dalian Barracuda Bay football Stadium caught my attention. On July 22, my uncle took me and my sister to the Barracuda Bay Football Stadium in Dalian. I’m so excited!

The wire mesh and membrane structure are the highlights of Barracuda Bay football Stadium. In order to meet the needs of lighting and ventilation, Dalian Barracuda Bay football stadium needs large span and large opening, and can not use the form of metal roof, so the flexible system of cable mesh and membrane material structure has become an appropriate choice.

From the “Dalian Daily”, I have learned that the structure of the cable network above the football field is like an umbrella bone, equivalent to the skeleton of the roof. The cable is a steel wire rope that has been rolled together, with three layers of a “Z” shape and vanadium plated on the outer ring to improve tension and corrosion protection. The roof cable net used in the football field weighs more than 2,600 tons, and the tension of a single cable exceeds 300 tons. The double-layer flexible cable net structure with upper oblique and lower radial is adopted for the first time in China.

Looking down on Barracuda Bay football Stadium from above, there will be a visual effect of choppy waves. It is learned from the Internet that this is because its awning is the first time in China to adopt a double-layer flexible cable network structure with upper oblique and lower radial, and is covered with ETFE film. The film material is modeled through the cable network structure to form a wave model. The facade is composed of ETFE cable membrane structure and flood lighting system around cable membrane. Among them, the facade adopts ETFE film of nearly 40,000 square meters, including 9 kinds of blue film materials, 7,823 diaphragms, which is the largest membrane structure coverage area of professional football stadiums in the world and the most single-layer ETFE membrane structure. Cable membrane structure, firm and reliable, good wind resistance. The membrane material not only makes the roof have a sense of layering, but also fully considers the roof drainage and other factors during installation, which can maximize the shade and rain for the audience and ensure the comfort of watching the game. Despite the wind blowing, the spectators can enjoy the game in the stadium.

The architecture of the Barracuda Bay football Stadium in Dalian is fantastic. The architectural wisdom contained in it shows the breadth and depth of physics, which amazes me a lot.
